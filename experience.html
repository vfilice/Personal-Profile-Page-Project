Experiences

Prior to pursuing a career in web development, I worked as a horticulturist/landscaper for many years. I began as a lowly apprentice and worked and studied my way up to become a foreman.

Key themes I learnt during that progression, the hard way most of the time, was to: stay humble, maintain a growth mindset, and recognize effort is the key to mastery.

I feel my previous path is very analogous to my current path and I am infinitely grateful to have those experiences to draw invaluable lessons from.
